Let's Talk About is a board game, carefully planned for a healthy communication. Having cues, hints, conversation beginners and topics that are carefully researched would help to satisfy the needs to develop a meaningful conversation. Individuals get to adopt a persona and choose how they would want to begin their conversation. The design is human sensitive and focuses on the overall problem of struggling on having a meaningful conversation with people from different contexts/ backgrounds.
