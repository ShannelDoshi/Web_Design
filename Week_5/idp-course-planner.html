This is a project for the Integrated design major at Parsons The New School for Design in New York. Essentially it is composed of a self-defining guide, a four year course planner, and pathway packages. All components aim to provide a solution for IDP students who struggle with planning their studies in a visual way. Integrated carriers are complex and there is a need for a design system that will organize them in a way that makes sense educationally. Working with Adam Brent, IDP Program Director at Parsons and Balbina Da Silva, a student at IDP, this projects designed as such to guide Integrated Design students towards defining their career, passions, pathways, and future endeavors through a system. The design is successfully been used by academic advisors, students, teachers and members of the IDP program team in order to explain the structure of the major more feasibly with a better visual manifestation.
