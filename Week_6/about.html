"I like to believe that my designs are not only created through various inspirations, but they are also a pure reflection of me and how I perceive and comprehend my world. It takes creativity to break the concrete in this abstract world and manifest myself and the universe I live in through my eyes" Currently based in New York City, Shannel Doshi desires to create her work highly inspired by the urban environment and lifestyle. Studying at Parsons The NewSchool of Design, she is completing her under graduation in Bachelors In Fine Arts, while majoring in Integrated Design. For her, the city and their people are her studio. During her initial interest in fashion design, she completed a summer course in 2013 at Central Saint Martins (UAL), in Fashion Sketchbook. She soon gained exposure to textiles where she completed a course in screen printing and textile designing at a block printing factory in Mumbai, India in 2014. Soon, her fascination towards video narration propelled her towards a Journalism course at Columbia University in May 2017. Her professional experiences range from working in non-profit organizations to designing and editing for commercial brands. For further information, feel free to contact under the 'contact' section.
